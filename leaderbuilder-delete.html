Delete Your Account

Last Updated: June 6, 2026

Leader Builder is a fly-fishing utility from Moonshinerxwerks LLC. You can delete your account and personal data at any time.

Delete from inside the app

  1. Open Settings.
  2. Tap Delete account.
  3. Confirm.

You'll be signed out immediately.

Can't access the app?

Email info@moonshinerxwerks.com from the email address associated with your account, and we'll delete it for you.

What gets deleted

When you delete your account, we permanently remove:

  • Your sign-in identity — your login and email address.
  • Your favorites.
  • Your personal notes — casting notes, recipe notes, and any other free text you wrote.

This removal is permanent and cannot be undone. Personal data is removed from our live database immediately and from backups within 30 days.

What we keep (anonymously)

The structured gear-pairing choices you created — which rod pairs with which line, leader, tip, and water conditions — are kept in de-identified form, with every link to you removed, to improve the shared catalog. This retained data contains no name, email, account identifier, or free text you wrote, and is not attributed to you. See Section 5 of our Privacy Policy for details.
