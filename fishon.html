FishON! Pro

Comprehensive fishing intelligence for the modern angler. FishON! Pro helps anglers decide where to fish today by aggregating real-time data from five federal agencies into a single, personalized intelligence platform.

📱 Platform iPhone · iPad
🏷️ Category Outdoors · Data · Intelligence

Features

📡

Live Multi-Agency Data

Real-time conditions from USGS, NOAA, NWS, Bureau of Reclamation, and NDBC buoys — flow rates, water temps, tides, weather, and wave data in one place.

🏆

Smart Scoring Engine

Ranks your fishing spots daily based on current conditions, your catch history, and field reports. The app learns your personal "golden conditions" over time.

📍

Three-Tier Location System

Organize all your water with a River → Section → Location hierarchy that mirrors how anglers actually think about their spots.

📝

Catch & Trip Logging

Log catches, trip reports, and field observations with automatic environmental snapshots that capture conditions at the moment you land a fish.

🗺️

Offline Satellite Maps

Full satellite maps for backcountry and remote water with no cell service. Download regions ahead of time and navigate without connectivity.

🎯

Where to Fish Today

Daily rankings from "best bet" to "skip today" that get smarter the more you fish — your own AI-powered fishing advisor.

🔒

Privacy-First Design

Your raw data never leaves your device. Your secret spots stay secret — we never share location or catch data with anyone.
