2. Description

FishON! Pro is a fishing intelligence platform that aggregates real-time environmental data from federal agencies (USGS, NOAA, NWS, Bureau of Reclamation, and NDBC) to help anglers decide where to fish. The App learns each angler's personal conditions over time and ranks fishing spots accordingly.
