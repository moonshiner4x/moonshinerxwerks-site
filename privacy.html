We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy or as required by law.

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal data, including the right to access, correct, delete, or port your data. To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.
