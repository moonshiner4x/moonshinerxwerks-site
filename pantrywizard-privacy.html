Pantry Wizard — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 6, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how moonshinerXwerks LLC collects, uses, and protects your information when you use Pantry Wizard. This policy is in addition to our general Privacy Policy.

2. Data We Collect

Ingredient data: Your pantry inventory, ingredient lists, and dietary preferences are stored locally on your device.

Cooking history: Your favorites and cooking history are stored on-device to improve recipe suggestions.

No personal data required: Pantry Wizard does not require an account to use. The App functions fully without collecting any personal information.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use collected data solely to provide and improve the App's features and functionality. We do not use your data for advertising or sell it to third parties.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on your device by default. If cloud sync features are used, data is encrypted and stored via Apple's CloudKit infrastructure. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information.

5. Third-Party Services

The App may use Apple's frameworks (CloudKit, StoreKit, MapKit) which are subject to Apple's privacy policy. We do not integrate third-party advertising SDKs or sell data to third parties.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

Your data remains on your device until you delete the App or clear its data. Cloud-synced data can be deleted through the App's settings. Upon account deletion or App removal, we do not retain copies of your personal data.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, and delete your data. Since most data is stored on-device, you have direct control. For any data stored in our systems, contact us to exercise your rights.

9. Changes

We may update this policy as the App evolves. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

10. Contact

For privacy questions, contact john@moonshinerxwerks.com.
