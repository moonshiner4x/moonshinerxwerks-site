Happy Hour Radar

A local discovery app that helps users find nearby venues with active happy hour deals. Built for Bend, Oregon, it combines real-time deal browsing, social planning, and crowd reporting into a seamless mobile experience.

📱 Platform iPhone · iPad
🏷️ Category Social · Local Discovery · Food & Drink

Features

🗺️

Venue Discovery on Map

Browse all local happy hour venues on an interactive map with custom logo pins, filterable by vibe (Chill, Craft Beer, Outdoor) and activities (Trivia, Live Music, Pool).

🔥

Live Deal Browsing

Filter venues to show only those with currently active happy hour deals, with deal details, timing, and crowd levels at a glance.

👥

Crowd Reporting

Users report and view real-time crowd density — Empty, Comfortable, Busy, or Packed — so you know what you're walking into.

🎉

Social Group Planning

Create plans at a venue, invite contacts, RSVP, and coordinate meetups with smart time defaults that snap to deal start times.

🔔

Proximity & Timed Alerts

Push notifications 15 minutes before a deal starts and 30 minutes before it ends, with location-based check-in prompts when you're nearby.

⭐

Visit Tracking & Favorites

Log check-ins, track visited venues, and save favorites. Build your personal map of Bend's best spots over time.

🏪

Business Owner Tools

Venue claiming, deal management, logo uploads, and real-time announcements — like "Just tapped a new keg" — to drive foot traffic.

💬

Ratings & Reviews

Star ratings with optional comments per venue so the community can surface the best happy hours in town.
