Cookie Policy

Last updated: March 6, 2026

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the site remember your preferences and understand how you use the site.

2. How We Use Cookies

moonshinerxwerks.com is a static website and uses minimal cookies. We may use:

  • Essential cookies: Required for basic site functionality such as form submissions.
  • Analytics cookies: To understand how visitors interact with our site, including page views and traffic sources. These help us improve our site.

3. Third-Party Cookies

Our site is hosted on Netlify, which may set cookies for performance and security purposes. We do not control third-party cookies and recommend reviewing Netlify's privacy policy for details.

4. Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies, delete existing cookies, or be notified when a cookie is set. Note that disabling cookies may affect site functionality.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy as our practices change. Updates will be posted on this page.
