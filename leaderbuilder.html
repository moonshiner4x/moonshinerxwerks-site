What it does

Keep a readily accessible and organized list of all line, tip and leader combinations that you use for every rod in every situation at your fingertips - approach that next run confident you have the right setup to get your fly in the target zone!

📖

Build leader recipes

A line + tip + leader, paired to a specific rod for specific water conditions (flow speed, target depth) and fly type.

✍️

Casting notes per recipe

Free-form memo for feel, anchor, loop shape, conditions where this rig shines. The note you'd write on the back of a fly box — searchable and synced.

🎣

Recall any recipe fast

Pick the rod you're fishing today and every recipe you've ever built for it is right there.

🧰

Curated catalog for recipe building

LDR | BLDR maintains a comprehensive list of readily available rods, lines, tips and leader building materials for you to quickly select from when configuring your recipes. If you don't see what you are looking for, add it and it will be there when you need it next!

📵

Works in the cell-dead canyon

Fully offline. Your recipes live on your device and sync up the next time you have a signal — because the spots you actually fish don't have 5G.

🔒

Privacy-first

No ads, no trackers, no analytics, no in-app purchases. Three sign-in options — Apple, Google, or email — so your recipes sync across your devices and nowhere else.
