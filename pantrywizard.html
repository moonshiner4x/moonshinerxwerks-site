Pantry Wizard

A beginner-focused cooking app that flips the traditional recipe search — instead of "what do you want to cook?", it starts with "what do you already have?" Enter your ingredients and get meals you can actually make tonight.

📱 Platform iPhone · iPad
🏷️ Category Cooking · Beginner-Friendly · Meal Planning

Features

🔍

Ingredient-to-Meal Matching

Enter what you have on hand and get ranked results showing full and partial matches — meals you can make right now with what's in your kitchen.

👨‍🍳

Step-by-Step Guidance

Beginner-friendly cooking instructions with troubleshooting tips at each step so you never feel lost in the kitchen.

🥗

Side Dish Suggestions

Get recommended sides with parallel cooking timelines so everything comes together at the same time.

🗄️

Pantry Management

Persistent ingredient tracking for your pantry and cupboard — add items once, and the app remembers what you have on hand.

❤️

Favorites & History

Save your go-to meals, browse your cooking history, and quickly re-make dishes you've loved before.

🥦

Dietary Preferences

Filter results by dietary needs — vegetarian, gluten-free, dairy-free, and more — so every suggestion fits your lifestyle.
