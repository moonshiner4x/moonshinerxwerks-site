2. Description of Service

Leader Builder is a fly-fishing utility that lets you:

  • Build, save, and revisit "recipes" — combinations of a line, leader, and optional tip paired to a specific rod and set of water conditions
  • Add casting notes to each recipe to capture how that rig fishes
  • Browse a studio-curated catalog of common rods, fly lines, leaders, tippet, and sink-tips so you can build recipes without typing every spec from scratch — and add custom entries when something's missing

The App is a personal-use tool. It is not professional fishing, casting, or safety advice, and we make no claims about the performance, suitability, or safety of any gear you record in it.
